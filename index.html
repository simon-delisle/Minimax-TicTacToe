Play Tic Tac Toe Against the Minimax Algorithm

Visualize the Minimax Score Associated with each Square for the AI Player Decision

  1. The minimax algorithm is used to assign a score to each square.
  2. The square with the highest score is chosen.
  3. If two squares have the same score, the first square (from top left to bottom right) is chosen.
    • A score of -10 represents losing the game if both players play best moves until the end of the game.
    • A score of 0 represents a tie game if both players play best moves.
    • A score of 10 represents a win if both players play best moves.
